Who am I?

My name is Lisa Cingolani and I am 20 years old. Currently a student, this website is a project I'm doing as part of my education in multimedia and Internet.

What's the purpose?

This blog is intended for the person I will become in a few decades. I have presented cultural elements that drew my attention and/or inspired me.

Further information

Mind_Uploading.exe is an imagined concept that, in my opinion, correspond to the problematic of the subject.

Indeed, "Uploading" is an upload to a server and the ".exe" ending designates an executable file, like an application. Therefore, the principle is simple. Using a software, you upload the contents of your mind, your memory, at a given time. This content is then exported to a server or any storage device.

This way, our memories and thoughts are not erased and are available at any time. That's exactly what this blog is, my personal backup.

Have a good read on Mind_Uploading.exe!
