FILMOGRAPHY

There are the different films directed by Spike Jonze, the author of the film Her.

  • Where the Wild Things are

    • This fantasy adventure film was released in 2009. Max is a little boy who has trouble dealing with his emotions. After a family argument, he decides to run away to an imaginary island. He will meet the Maximonsters, and become their king. This experience will make him realize that he must change his attitude towards the world.

    With Max Records, Catherine Keener, Mark Ruffalo.

  • Being John Malkovich

    • This 1999 film is a dramatic and fantastic comedy. Craig Schwartz has just been hired in the Lester company. In his office, he finds a door, leading to the unconscious of John Malkovich for fifteen minutes. This discovery will change his life.

    With John Cusack, Cameron Diaz, John Malkovich.

  • Adaptation

    • This film is a comedy-drama from 2003. While working on the adaptation on screen of an original book, screenwriter Charlie Kaufman has to face a major existential crisis.

    With Nicolas Cage, Tilda Swinton, Meryl Streep.
