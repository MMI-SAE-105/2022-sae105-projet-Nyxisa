OF ANDROIDS AND MEN

Android:
A robot that looks like a human being.
Empathy:
Understanding and sympathizing with the emotions of other people.
Martyr:
A person who has suffered or died for a particular cause.
Tetanus:
A serious, often fatal disease caused by bacteria found in soil. It enters the body through a wound and attacks the nervous system.
Hypodermic needle:
A hollow needle used with a syringe to inject or withdraw substances from the blood.

LIVING IN A MACHINE

Brutalism:
This architectural movement consists of the use of raw concrete for imposing buildings. Another characteristic of brutalism is the repetition of elements, such as numerous windows.
Bauhaus:
It is one of the most important artistic movements of the 20th century. Originally, it was a school that combined the learning of fine arts and crafts. Vassily Kandinsky and Paul Klee were teachers there. Many of modern architecture foundations come directly from the Bauhaus.
Duplex:
A two-level flat with an internal staircase. This arrangement can be quite original and give a feeling of extra space.
Loggia:
A covered outdoor space that is set back from the facade, thus facing the interior of the building. This space can be closed by a bay window.
